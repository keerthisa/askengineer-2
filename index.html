VTU vs AUTONOMUS

VTU is a better choice than autonomous. Autonomous colleges are those colleges that are affiliated to some university (most colleges are affiliated to VTU) but form their own syllabus and conduct their own exam. However, the degree is still awarded by VTU. Most other colleges follow the VTU syllabus and exam.

Coding

The "code" we write is in languages called high level languages (higher = closer to human languages, lower = closer to machine language/binary). There is another program called a compiler (or an interpreter) that takes your English-like code and converts it into something that the computer can understand ("machine language"). Machine Language is basically a 1-1 translation from binary instructions that you can give to the computer (basically human readable binary instructions.

Interviews

The thing with interviews is that they look effortless. It feels like it won't be a big deal, but that you will be spontaneously pitching for an opportunity while facing a new person or, in some cases, a team is a little scary. The interview is about more than just you, so preparing yourself for it is essential. It's about whether or not you're a good fit for the company. So, in addition to preparing yourself, take some time to think about how you can show that you're the right person for the job.
